Mosquito Sucker: The innovative device turning pest control into a game

Mosquito Sucker is a fully automatic mosquito-sucking gun independently developed by the Master of Mosquito team. The mosquito-sucking gun is similar in structure to a traditional pistol. It generates a large amount of suction through a ducted design, which can easily suck in a mosquito with a diameter of 0.1 cm from a certain distance, eliminating much of the fatigue involved in the traditional process of swatting and killing mosquitoes.

The mosquito-suction gun is equipped with a 12-volt high-powered fan inside, leaving no mosquitoes a chance to escape. Through connections of independently soldered electronic components, a 6000mAh high-capacity battery provides strong power to the gun. All mosquitoes sucked into the muzzle will be stuck in the filter screen placed in front of the air outlet, and finally perish in the strong wind resembling the eye of a typhoon.

Although it is not as efficient or simple as mosquito control methods such as electric mosquito swatters and mosquito-killing lamps, we believe that its excellent performance and highly creative mosquito-catching path have endowed Mosquito Sucker with a unique value. All users will have a relaxed and interesting experience in the process of mosquito control, making the annoying task of swatting mosquitoes, which is stereotypically perceived as such, feel like a game. This is our team's design philosophy:

Looking at the world from a different perspective, perhaps the complicated and annoying things can turn into a pleasant enjoyment.
